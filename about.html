About

Hello.

I'm Calvin Ip, a digital artist and UX designer who is passionate about designing simple yet expressive experiences. My favourite aspect of which, is translating complex processes and user journeys to simple and easy to use formats.

Having worked freelance in multiple different design and art projects, I knew the pain of designing without prior user and business knowledge. This often led to submitting a final design to clients, who would give never-ending feedback and iterations.

A month in, I realised that this was an inefficient way at approaching the design process and decided to incorporate user research into my design. Like many aspiring designers, I spent time reading about different design processes, both in and out of the field.

With my background in Bachelor in Aerospace Engineering, I learnt the importance in iterating designs to reach optimal and desired results. As a designer, employing the merits of iterations is a continuous process and I am still learning more effective ways to iterate within new projects.

In my downtime, I enjoy experimenting and cooking new dishes as well as participating in different sports with friends or family.
