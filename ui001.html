Daily UI 001: Sign Up

Create a sign up page, modal, form, or app screen related to signing up for something. It could be for a volunteer event, contest registration, a giveaway, or anything you can image.

Design Changes:

  • Combine sign up and log in functionality, nudging up the title to improve hierarchy.
  • Adding a friendly introduction.
  • Adding a tinted background on the input field to improve hierarchy.
  • Adding colour to the CTA to show importance.
  • Reduce friction by introducing social sign in with Google or Apple
  • Make it softer using shadows and rounding the corners.
